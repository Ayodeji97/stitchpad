What to weigh
  • Burgundy is a perennial fashion color — feels premium and refined.
  • Risk: lives in the same red family as the Overdue status pill. On a dashboard with an overdue order, the brand color and the warning color can read as “two reds shouting.”
  • Less culturally specific than indigo — wine says “premium fashion house” (anywhere), not “Nigerian tailor heritage.”
