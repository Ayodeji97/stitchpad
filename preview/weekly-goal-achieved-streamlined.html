What I cut from B and why:
· "Weekly target exceeded" check row — the strikethrough ₦300k already says it.
· "₦700,000 earned · Target ₦300,000" footer — same numbers as the title row, said differently.
· Edit goal → Raise your goal — at this moment the user has proven they can take more; preserve momentum.

What I kept from B:
· Trophy in a circle badge — keeps the celebratory hero feel.
· Big strikethrough ₦700k / ₦300k — does the work two rows in B did.
· Days-left as a saffron pill (vs. plain text in A) — pulls more weight in the header.

Net: ~180dp vs Canva's ~290dp. Still ~1.4× the current compact (132dp), but reads as a hero — not as a status row.
