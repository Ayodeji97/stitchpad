Proposal for PTSP-XX. Renames "Garment type" → "What are you making?" and "Description (optional)" → "Design notes (optional)", and replaces the closed-enum dropdown with a search-enabled bottom-sheet picker that persists custom garment names per tailor. Five states below show the full flow from rename → search → add custom → re-use.

Open design questions (for the brainstorm)
  • Sort order for "My garment types": Usage-count desc (shown here) vs. most-recent vs. alphabetical. Usage-count rewards real workflow but hides new customs.
  • Where to store custom values: Subcollection users/{uid}/customGarmentTypes/{id} vs. an array on the User doc. Subcollection scales; array is simpler and atomic.
  • Case + dedupe: "Iro and Buba" vs. "iro and buba" — store normalized lowercase, display original casing? Or warn on near-duplicates ("Did you mean Iro and Buba?")?
  • "Custom" badge everywhere: Should the badge follow the value to the order detail screen and the receipt, or only show in the form? (Lean: only in form, since detail/receipt is for the customer.)
  • Empty state on first launch: A brand-new tailor has zero customs. Show the "My garment types" section as empty with a hint, or hide it entirely until they have one? (Lean: hide until first add.)
  • Migration: Existing OrderItems all have garmentType: enum. New field customGarmentName: String? defaults to null — no backfill needed. Confirm receipt-grouping aggregates by custom name when set.
