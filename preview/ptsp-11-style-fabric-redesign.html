PTSP-11 — Multi-image Style & Fabric, redesigned

Three directions for the redesigned Style and Fabric sections of the per-item card on the order form. Each phone shows the same step (Step 2 of 3 — Items) with the same content state: Style filled with 2 images (1 from saved library + 1 uploaded) and Fabric filled with 1 uploaded image, plus the +tile to add more (max 3 per category).

Bundled fixes in all three: "Pick from gallery" renamed to "Choose from saved styles" so it can't be confused with the phone's photo gallery. Button hides entirely when the customer has zero saved styles (Variant A's right card simulates this — fabric has no library concept, so it's always upload-only).

Claude's pick
Variant B+

Inline structure + chip affordance

B's flat hierarchy (no nested cards) with stronger button chips below the strip. Photo strip is the focus; action chips are unmistakable when the row is empty or has space.
