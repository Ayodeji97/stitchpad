Order details — V2 redesign

Five states of the same screen, redesigned to match the dashboard V2 illustrated-stack language. Hero card answers "what is this and what do I do next?" in one glance. Delete moves out of the bottom CTA (where it didn't belong) into the ⋮ overflow. The Notes card takes its place — high-value, missing today, and finally editable inline. The 6-stage production timeline shows how tailors actually narrate work.

What changes vs. today's screen

↳Delete — moved from bottom CTA to ⋮ overflow (with confirmation)
+Notes card — takes Delete's old spot; tap to edit inline
+Hero card — fabric thumb, status, balance, due, dual CTA in one block
+Customer chips — WhatsApp · Call · Measurements one tap away
+6-stage timeline — Pending · Cutting · Sewing · Fitting · Ready · Delivered
+Payment history — line items with method + date
↳Priority — moved into garment card as a pill (was its own section)
−Generic SectionHeader labels — replaced by per-card icon tile + title
