Sewing status colour — current vs. proposed

Today the Sewing order-status chip is painted with the exact same hex as the brand primary (#E8A800). That means a saffron pixel on a Pipeline card can mean either “StitchPad brand” or “this order is being sewn.” The proposed change moves Sewing to a deep indigo (#5B4D8E) — the traditional Nigerian textile counterpart to saffron — so brand identity and workflow semantics never share a colour.
